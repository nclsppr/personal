HTTP 504 - GATEWAY_TIMEOUT

Gateway timeout.

The upstream server took too long to answer, and the gateway gave up waiting.

Le serveur en amont a mis trop de temps à répondre, la passerelle a cessé d’attendre.

nicolas@pieper.fr - zsh
$ curl --max-time 30 http://upstream:8080/
curl: (28) Operation timed out after 30000 ms
$ echo $?
504
