What I bring#

I started as a developer and still use that technical background every day as a manager. I set direction, make ownership visible and help the team work through business constraints without losing sight of production.

  • Lead and grow autonomous engineering teams with clear ownership
  • Turn business needs, constraints and risk into clear technical roadmaps
  • Design and modernize secure, scalable platforms without ignoring legacy or production
  • Improve delivery, CI/CD and operational reliability across products

I am most useful when a system is critical, the technical choices are not obvious and several teams need to move together.

Beyond engineering#

When I am not working on platforms or engineering organizations, I am probably building a home automation nobody asked for, testing a new sensor, walking through Lorraine or the Vosges with Pampy, my Golden Retriever, or stretching dough for a homemade Neapolitan pizza after a 72-hour cold ferment. I enjoy technology, but I am mostly interested in what people and teams can build with it, ideally over a good pizza.

Let's talk.

If you are working on a secure platform, a difficult modernization or an engineering organization that needs clearer ownership, I would be glad to compare notes.
